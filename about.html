Hi, I'm Cheemaladari Revanth

Semester Exchange Student | Explorer & Developer

Hello! I am Cheemaladari Revanth, a semester exchange student currently studying at INTI International University, Nilai, Malaysia. I am originally from India, and this incredible journey has given me the unique opportunity to experience a brand-new culture, educational system, and biodiversity.

During my period of study here, my goal is not just academic excellence, but also to deeply explore the stunning landscapes of Malaysia. From the towering peaks of Mount Kinabalu to the ancient dipterocarp rainforests of Taman Negara, I am documenting my travels to share the essence of true eco-tourism.

This website was built as a passion project to help guide fellow travelers, students, and nature enthusiasts on how we can all experience the world responsibly. Join me on my journey as we uncover Malaysia's wild heart!

What is Eco Tourism?

Eco-tourism is defined as "responsible travel to natural areas that conserves the environment, sustains the well-being of the local people, and involves interpretation and education."

Environmental Benefits: It provides a direct financial incentive for governments to protect habitats. By engaging local communities, it transforms them into dedicated stewards of their local ecology who protect wildlife rather than resort to poaching.
